OL3 example: Pseudo-mercator projection (EPSG:3857)

Swissimage (20151231)

New national map (20151231)

This examples shows how to use geo.admin.ch tiles in a map using the pseudo-mercator projection (EPSG:3857).

The WMTS tiles from Pixelkarte and Swissimage are reprojected on-the-fly from the original source, which is in LV03 (EPSG:21781).

Have a look at what projections are available.
